title: "Katharsis"
description: "Poem heavily inspired by 'Katharsis' by Toru Kitajima, the lead singer of Ling Tosite Sigure. The poem explores themes of rebirth and transformation in the face of an apocalyptic event."
type: "Poetry"

pubDate: 2025-04-06

If I could open eyes and ears,
Would ***“just a dream”*** be what I hear?
You drift into my restless view—
A miracle I never knew.

But why can't we let the past find peace?
The ghost of guilt will not release.
I ran from sins I shaped alone,
Then clawed through ash to find my soul.

I longed to wreck what once felt right,
To turn love’s day into the night.
I pointed fingers at the sky,
But found the blade within my grip—
And still, I lie.

So draw it out and lay me bare,
Undo this form, strip every layer.
'Til only echoes fill the air,
And sorrow whispers: ***“I miss you there.”***

So sentence me again, again—
Strike every fracture, break what’s left.
Rip me apart at all the seams,
Until I'm nothing, not even dreams.

And then—
Would you love me?
Would you let it go?
Would you raise me up,
Or let me fall below?

Now, will it be me?
It **has** to be me.
It can’t be me.

**You’ll never light my way.**
