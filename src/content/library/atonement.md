---
title: "Would You Love Me If I Atoned?"
description: "Poem drawing heavily from my own experiences of abandonment, rejection and struggles of repairing sins and mistakes of my own creation."
type: "Poetry"
tags: ["poem", "melancholy", "angst", "atonement"]
pubDate: 2025-03-23
---

If I silenced a voice, stole a breath away,
Would you call me a friend, or turn in dismay?
Would you see my hands, now tainted and red,
And still stand beside me, or wish I were dead?

I swore I’d protect you, no matter the cost,
But in saving your world, my own self was lost.
The weight of the act drags me down like a stone,
And yet here I stand, afraid and alone.

If I could undo it, erase what I’ve done,
Would you stay by my side, or would you still run?
